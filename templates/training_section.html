Instructions

  1. Options:

    1. 1. If you have a photo of the neck (including the upper back) taken already proceed to the section below labaled Using the Web Application.
    2. 2. If you do not have the photo The following instructions will be for you.

  2. Preparation:

    1. a. Another person is required for this process (this person will be taking the photo for you).
    2. b. The following instructions will be for the other person.
    3. b. Ensure that the person you are taking the photo is comfortable and properly positioned, preferably sitting, or standing upright.
    4. c. Provide adequate lighting in the room to ensure clear visibility of the neck area.
    5. d. Make sure the person's hair is pulled away from the neck to avoid obstruction.

  3. Camera Setup:

    1. a. Open the camera application on your cell phone.
    2. b. Ensure that the camera lens is clean and free from any smudges or dirt.

  4. Capturing the Photo:

    1. a. Position the camera at the level of the patient's neck, parallel to the ground.
    2. b. Ensure the entire area of interest is centered and clearly visible in the camera frame.
    3. c. Once the area is in focus and properly framed, gently press the shutter button to take the photo.
    4. d. Avoid sudden movements while capturing the image to prevent blurring
    5. e. Review and Retake (if necessary):

  5. Using the Web Application:

    1. a. Proceed to the homepage.
    2. b. Press the Browse button and select the picture that was now taken.
    3. c. Proceed to click the upload button.
    4. d. Await for Prediction
